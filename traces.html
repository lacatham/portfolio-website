Traces & Places

Concept

Traces & Place is a location-based responsive Web app aimed at family historians and cultural heritage specialists who want to collect data and events about the historical past.

The idea: address commonly understood users’ problems while offering a new radically different perspective, tapping into users’ unmet needs and paints points.

  • Role: UX & UI Designer
  • Period: august-September 2020
  • Tools: Sketch, InVision

Scope:

  • UI design
  • Mobile & Responsive Web

Objective

  • Allow users to identify, localize, and describe places in time and space, and gather that information in a single form.
  • Period: august-September 2020
  • Capture the process through which users produce records & new knowledge.
