Bonjour

I'm Myriam, I work on product design for Anaphore, a software company based in Provence, France.

Anaphore has been building digital solutions for professional archivists for over twenty years. We help professionals carry their mission, and we facilitate access to archival content for the general public.

Formally trained as a historian and as an archivist, I have come to UI design as a natural path evolution to enrich my missions. For over ten years I have trained and accompanied our clients on our digital tools within the context of archival practice.

As managing director and consultant, I have acquired a keen understanding of people’s needs to accomplish their mission. I am passionate about integrating design thinking principles with business, market, and user insights to allow the team to deliver innovative and impactful solutions.

As a creative person I have always been found of everything design and love to solve problems that can be expressed in a digital solution.

I conduct freelance work when the project offers challenges I feel I can contribute to solve.
